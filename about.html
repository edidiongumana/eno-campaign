Career


Barr. Emmanuel Enoidem began his career as a Lecturer at the University of Uyo, Uyo in 1999. He began his political career in 2002. Barr. Enoidem served as a Local Government Chairman of Etim Ekpo Local Government Area, Treasurer of The People's Democratic Party (PDP),
Akwa Ibom State for 6 and a half years, Commissioner for Housing and Urban Renewal for eight years in the tenure of former Akwa Ibom state governor, Godswill Akpabio, where he interpreted the dream of the then administration that saw to the development of different sectors of the state.

He also held the portfolio of Commissioner of Commerce, Industries, and Investment in the administration of Governor Udom Emmanuel where he dedicated himself to the service of interpreting the Dakadda philosophy of his Boss, Governor Udom Emmanuel, and laying a foundation for the commerce sector to thrive.
Having gathered these experiences, he went on to become the National Legal Adviser of the People's Democratic Party (PDP).
